# How to Enable Bluetooth on VibeKeypad

This guide will walk you through enabling Bluetooth connectivity on your VibeKeypad using the SayoDevice configuration tool.

## Prerequisites

- Google Chrome browser (required)
- USB-C cable
- Your VibeKeypad device

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Open the Configuration Tool

Navigate to [https://sayodevice.com/](https://sayodevice.com/) in Google Chrome.

> **Note:** Chrome is required because it supports WebHID API, which is necessary for device configuration.

### 2. Connect Your Device

Connect your VibeKeypad to your computer using a USB-C cable.

When prompted by Chrome, select your device from the list and click **Connect**.

### 3. Configure RF Settings

1. In the SayoDevice interface, locate and click on **RF Settings**
2. Find the connectivity mode dropdown
3. Select **BLE&Auto** from the available options
4. Click **Save** or **Apply** to write the settings to your device

### 4. Disconnect and Pair

1. **Disconnect** the USB cable from your VibeKeypad
2. Open your operating system's **Bluetooth settings**:
   - **macOS**: System Settings → Bluetooth
   - **Windows**: Settings → Bluetooth & devices
   - **Linux**: Settings → Bluetooth
3. Click **Add New Device** or **Pair new device**
4. Select **VibeKeypad** from the list of available devices
5. Click **Connect** or **Pair**
